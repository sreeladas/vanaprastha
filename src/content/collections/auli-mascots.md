This mascot sculpture originates from Auli, a prominent ski destination in Uttarakhand. It is associated with the early development of winter sports in the region, particularly around the 5th National Winter Games held in 1993. The form reflects a locally rooted visual language, possibly influenced by folk and handcrafted aesthetics. While detailed records are limited, the mascot represents a significant period in Auli's emergence as a centre for skiing and winter recreation. It stands as a visual marker of the region's sporting heritage and cultural identity during that time.
