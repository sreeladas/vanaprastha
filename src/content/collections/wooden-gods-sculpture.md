This section features carved wooden sculptures of deities from the Vanaprastha collection. The pieces include a Rama and Sita sculpture from Bali, Indonesia, crafted in Suar wood, and a Bayon-style multi-faced head sculpture from Cambodia in teak wood. These works reflect the rich tradition of devotional woodcarving across Southeast Asia.
