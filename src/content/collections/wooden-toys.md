The Vanaprastha Wooden Toy Collection presents a charming ensemble of handcrafted wooden toys rooted in India's rich artistic and spiritual traditions. Carefully selected from the personal collection of Surjit Kishore Das, these pieces reflect a deep appreciation for cultural storytelling, craftsmanship, and heritage. Each toy features intricate woodwork, delicately carved idols of deities, and traditional Pattachitra paintings that bring mythological and devotional themes to life.

Beyond their visual appeal, these toys represent a deeper connection to India's folk practices and ritual objects. Thoughtfully designed wooden storage boxes accompany many of the pieces, adding both utility and an added layer of elegance. Together, the toys and their cases offer a unique blend of playfulness, reverence, and design — capturing the spirit of Vanaprastha as a space of quiet reflection and aesthetic richness.
