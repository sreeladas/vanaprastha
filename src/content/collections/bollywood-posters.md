A curated selection of Bollywood photographic posters from the Vanaprastha collection. Created using real studio photography and cinematic visuals, these posters offer a glimpse into the evolving aesthetics of Indian film promotion. Through powerful imagery, expressive portraits, and iconic layouts, each poster captures the mood, star presence, and storytelling style of its time - an exploration of Bollywood's visual legacy through its most direct and impactful medium.
