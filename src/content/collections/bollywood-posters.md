A curated selection of Bollywood photographic posters from the Vanaprastha collection. These posters, created using real studio photography and cinematic visuals, offer a glimpse into the evolving aesthetics of Indian film promotion. Through powerful imagery, expressive portraits, and iconic layouts, each poster captures the mood, star presence, and storytelling style of its time. This collection invites viewers to explore the visual legacy of Bollywood through its most direct and impactful medium — the photographic film poster.
