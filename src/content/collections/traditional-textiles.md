Beautifully hand-crafted textiles drawing from tribal art traditions such as Bhil, Warli, and Pithora painting. The pieces include Sujani embroidery from Bihar, Pithora painting from Gujarat, and mirror-and-patchwork textiles from Rajasthan. Each piece showcases intricate craftsmanship, bold patterns, and vibrant colors, reflecting the deep-rooted relationship between indigenous art and everyday life.
