A compelling ensemble of works by artists such as Rathin Mitra, Nicholas Roerich, and Dr. Y. Mathpal, along with photographs captured by Surjit Das himself. Rooted in the visual language of ritual, landscape, and symbolic expression, these works reflect a wide range of spiritual, emotional, and cultural resonances.

Each piece offers a window into deeper artistic journeys - exploring identity, devotion, contemplation, and transformation. More than decorative artworks, they are silent narrators of stories, moods, and inner worlds - echoes of heritage, imagination, and introspection.
