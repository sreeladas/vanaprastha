More than just a surface element, this humble tile holds a deeply personal story. Once a part of the original flooring of the house, it witnessed the everyday life, footsteps, and quiet moments that shaped the space over decades. Carefully preserved by Late S.K. Das, the tile reflects not just his attention to detail but also his emotional connection to the home and its architectural character. It speaks of a time gone by — of craftsmanship, material memory, and a life rooted in cultural values. Today, it stands as a small yet powerful symbol of the house's legacy, echoing stories of belonging, care, and timelessness.
