Framed photographs taken by Surjit Das, reflecting his deep sensitivity toward landscapes, history, and cultural memory. Among the highlights is a striking aerial photograph captured during his return journey from Bhutan to Uttarakhand, offering a rare glimpse of the Himalayan range from above. Also featured is a framed 1953 London Herald front page commemorating the first successful ascent of Mount Everest - a testament to his interest in historic milestones. Each photograph stands as a visual document of a life lived with curiosity, reflection, and an enduring connection to place and time.
