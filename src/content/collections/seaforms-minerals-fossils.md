The Vanaprastha collection presents a captivating array of sea shells, corals, minerals, and rocks that reflect the rich diversity and beauty of the natural world. This curated assemblage offers an insightful glimpse into marine and geological formations, showcasing intricate textures, unique shapes, and vivid colors shaped by nature over time. The collection serves as both an aesthetic and educational resource, inviting viewers to explore the intersection of art, science, and natural history.
